Survival Guide for Participants

Follow these key steps to make the most of your SIGMA26 experience

1
Registration
Arrive at 08:00 on February 10. Complete registration at the main entrance. Collect your badge and documentation.
2
Opening Ceremony
Join the opening plenary session at 09:00. Listen to institutional addresses and keynote speakers.
3
Coffee Break & Networking
Connect with peers during breaks. View poster presentations in parallel sessions.
4
Technical Sessions
Attend oral presentations in your field. Sessions run in parallel across multiple rooms. Check schedules carefully.
5
Lunch Break
Enjoy provided meals from 13:00-14:15. Continue networking with colleagues.
6
Closing & Gala
Day 1 ends at 18:15. Gala dinner at 20:00. Day 2 ends with distribution of certificates at 17:00, And a dinner at 20:30.
Important Info: Sessions are held in 3 main rooms: Conference Room (Library), Department of Industrial Engineering, and Mediatheque Room. Each presentation lasts 15 minutes. Be punctual!
